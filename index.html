Cubeworld (Working Title) by smacdo

You wake up in a mysterious dream world made entirely of cubes, and must discover a way back home!

Cubeworld is a first person sandbox game in which you live in a world filled with cubes. You can interact with these cubes in different ways, break them, reform them into items and explore large randomly generated worlds full of danger and reward! To see what features we've done, and are doing, see: http://goo.gl/cXe2

Dependencies
